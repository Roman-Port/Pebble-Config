iOS Setup Step 1
While it is optional, I recommend reinstalling the Pebble app before starting. Pebble seems to heavily cache these things.
iOS Setup Step 2
Once that is done, go into the settings menu. In a blank spot, (somewhere without a button) tap five times with three fingers. This will pull up the hidden debug menu.
iOS Setup Step 3
Inside of this menu, tap the "Debug Settings" button.
iOS Setup Step 4
In the next menu, scroll to the bottom of the page. Tap the "Custom Remote Config Endpoint" button.
iOS Setup Step 5
In the text box, type the URL below. Then, press the okay button.
Copy URL
iOS Setup Step 6
Now, back out of all of the menu options until you're on the home page of the Pebble app. Now, exit the app and slide it out of the multitasking view. Then, hold the power button down but do not shutdown! Wait until the shutdown menu appears, then hold the home button until you are returned to the home screen. This will clear the cache and (hopefully) force the Pebble app to reload the config.
